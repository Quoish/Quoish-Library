#场论 
# Lame系数
Lame系数可以方便地表示直角坐标系和各种正交曲线坐标系的转换关系，其计算式如下
$$H_i = \sqrt{\left(\frac{\partial x}{\partial q_i}\right)^2+\left(\frac{\partial y}{\partial q_i}\right)^2+\left(\frac{\partial z}{\partial q_i}\right)^2}\ \ \ \ \ \ i=1,2,3$$
## 微分元
### 弧微分
$$\mathrm{d}s_i = H_i \mathrm{d}q_i\ \ \ \ \ \ i=1,2,3$$
### 面微分
$$\mathrm{d}\sigma_{ij} = H_i H_j \mathrm{d}q_i \mathrm{d}q_j\ \ \ \ \ \ i,j=1,2,3且i\neq k$$
### 体微分
$$\mathrm{d}V = H_1 H_2 H_3 \mathrm{d}q_1\mathrm{d}q_2\mathrm{d}q_3$$
## 标准正交基
$$\vec{e_i} = \frac{1}{H_i}\frac{\partial x}{\partial q_i}\vec{i}+ \frac{1}{H_i}\frac{\partial y}{\partial q_i}\vec{j}+ \frac{1}{H_i}\frac{\partial z}{\partial q_i}\vec{k} \ \ \ \ \ i=1,2,3$$
## 梯散旋
### [[Nabla算子]]
$$\nabla = 
\frac{\vec{e_1}}{H_1}\frac{\partial}{\partial q_1}+
\frac{\vec{e_2}}{H_2}\frac{\partial}{\partial q_2}+
\frac{\vec{e_3}}{H_3}\frac{\partial}{\partial q_3}
$$
### 梯度
$$\nabla u =
\frac{\vec{e_1}}{H_1}\frac{\partial u}{\partial q_1}+
\frac{\vec{e_2}}{H_2}\frac{\partial u}{\partial q_2}+
\frac{\vec{e_3}}{H_3}\frac{\partial u}{\partial q_3}$$
### 散度
$$\nabla\cdot \vec{A} =
\frac{1}{H_1H_2H_3}
\left[
\frac{\partial }{\partial q_1}(H_2H_3A_1)+
\frac{\partial }{\partial q_2}(H_3H_1A_2)+
\frac{\partial }{\partial q_3}(H_1H_2A_3)
\right]$$
### 旋度
$$\nabla\times \vec{A} =
\frac{1}{H_1H_2H_3}
\left|
\begin{matrix}
H_1\vec{e_1}&H_2\vec{e_2}&H_3\vec{e_3}\\
\frac{\partial}{\partial q_1}&\frac{\partial}{\partial q_2}&\frac{\partial}{\partial q_3}\\
H_1A_1&H_2A_2&H_3A_3\\
\end{matrix}
\right|$$
> 注意：这个行列式不是严格的行列式，只是一个记忆式，若要展开必须按第一行展开，否则微分算子的非交换性将可能导致计算结果错误。
### Laplace算子
$$\Delta=
\frac{1}{H_1H_2H_3}
\left[
\frac{\partial }{\partial q_1}(\frac{H_2H_3}{H_1}\frac{\partial}{\partial q_1})+
\frac{\partial }{\partial q_2}(\frac{H_3H_1}{H_2}\frac{\partial}{\partial q_2})+
\frac{\partial }{\partial q_3}(\frac{H_1H_2}{H_3}\frac{\partial}{\partial q_3})
\right]$$
# 直角坐标系
## 梯度
$$\nabla f = \frac{\partial f}{\partial x} \mathbf{e}_x + \frac{\partial f}{\partial y} \mathbf{e}_y + \frac{\partial f}{\partial z} \mathbf{e}_z$$
## 散度
$$\nabla \cdot \mathbf{A} = \frac{\partial A_x}{\partial x} + \frac{\partial A_y}{\partial y} + \frac{\partial A_z}{\partial z}$$
## 旋度
$$\nabla \times \mathbf{A} = 
\begin{vmatrix}
\mathbf{e}_x & \mathbf{e}_y & \mathbf{e}_z \\
\frac{\partial}{\partial x} & \frac{\partial}{\partial y} & \frac{\partial}{\partial z} \\
A_x & A_y & A_z
\end{vmatrix}$$
## 调和量
$$\nabla^2 f = \frac{\partial^2 f}{\partial x^2} + \frac{\partial^2 f}{\partial y^2} + \frac{\partial^2 f}{\partial z^2}$$
# 柱坐标系
## 梯度
$$\nabla f = \frac{\partial f}{\partial \rho} \mathbf{e}_\rho + \frac{1}{\rho} \frac{\partial f}{\partial \phi} \mathbf{e}_\phi + \frac{\partial f}{\partial z} \mathbf{e}_z$$
## 散度
$$\nabla \cdot \mathbf{A} = \frac{1}{\rho} \frac{\partial}{\partial \rho} (\rho A_\rho) + \frac{1}{\rho} \frac{\partial A_\phi}{\partial \phi} + \frac{\partial A_z}{\partial z}$$
## 旋度
$$\nabla \times \mathbf{A} = \frac{1}{\rho} \begin{vmatrix} \mathbf{e}_\rho & \rho \mathbf{e}_\phi & \mathbf{e}_z \\ \frac{\partial}{\partial \rho} & \frac{\partial}{\partial \phi} & \frac{\partial}{\partial z} \\ A_\rho & \rho A_\phi & A_z \end{vmatrix}$$
## 调和量
$$\nabla^2 f = \frac{1}{\rho} \frac{\partial}{\partial \rho} \left( \rho \frac{\partial f}{\partial \rho} \right) + \frac{1}{\rho^2} \frac{\partial^2 f}{\partial \phi^2} + \frac{\partial^2 f}{\partial z^2}$$
# 球坐标系
## 梯度
$$\nabla f = \frac{\partial f}{\partial r} \mathbf{e}_r + \frac{1}{r} \frac{\partial f}{\partial \theta} \mathbf{e}_\theta + \frac{1}{r \sin \theta} \frac{\partial f}{\partial \phi} \mathbf{e}_\phi
$$
## 散度
$$\nabla \cdot \mathbf{A} = \frac{1}{r^2} \frac{\partial}{\partial r} (r^2 A_r) + \frac{1}{r \sin \theta} \frac{\partial}{\partial \theta} (\sin \theta A_\theta) + \frac{1}{r \sin \theta} \frac{\partial A_\phi}{\partial \phi}$$
## 旋度
$$\nabla \times \mathbf{A} = \frac{1}{r^2 \sin \theta} \begin{vmatrix} \mathbf{e}_r & r \mathbf{e}_\theta & r \sin \theta \mathbf{e}_\phi \\ \frac{\partial}{\partial r} & \frac{\partial}{\partial \theta} & \frac{\partial}{\partial \phi} \\ A_r & r A_\theta & r \sin \theta A_\phi \end{vmatrix}$$
## 调和量
$$\nabla^2 f = \frac{1}{r^2} \frac{\partial}{\partial r} \left( r^2 \frac{\partial f}{\partial r} \right) + \frac{1}{r^2 \sin \theta} \frac{\partial}{\partial \theta} \left( \sin \theta \frac{\partial f}{\partial \theta} \right) + \frac{1}{r^2 \sin^2 \theta} \frac{\partial^2 f}{\partial \phi^2}$$
