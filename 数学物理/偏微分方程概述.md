# 三大经典偏微分方程

## [[波动方程]]
$$
\frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial t^2} = a^2\nabla^2 u
$$
## [[热传导方程]]
$$
\frac{\partial u}{\partial t} = a^2\nabla^2 u
$$
## [[位势方程]]
$$
c = \nabla^2 u
$$

# Sterm-Liouville标准形的化法
$$
\begin{align}
&a(x) \frac{d^2 y}{dx^2} + b(x) \frac{dy}{dx} + c(x)y + \lambda d(x)y = 0\\
&\mu(x) = \exp\left( \int \frac{b(x) - a'(x)}{a(x)}  dx \right)\\
&p(x) = \mu(x) a(x), \quad q(x) = \mu(x) c(x), \quad w(x) = \mu(x) d(x)\\
&\frac{d}{dx}\left[p(x) \frac{dy}{dx}\right] + q(x)y + \lambda w(x)y = 0\\
\end{align}
$$
